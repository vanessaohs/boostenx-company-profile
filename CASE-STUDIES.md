## Case Study 1: Regulated Forex Broker — Reputation Recovery & Growth

### Client Profile
A mid-size regulated forex broker based in the UAE, serving retail and institutional clients across MENA. The broker held valid regulatory licenses but was being targeted by competitors using fake negative reviews to damage their reputation.

### Challenge
- Over 200 fake or misleading negative reviews across major review platforms
- Trust score had dropped 40% over six months, directly impacting new client acquisition
- Manual review flagging process was slow and inconsistent, with platforms taking weeks to respond
- Marketing team spending 15+ hours per week on manual reputation monitoring

### BoostenX Solution
The client deployed BoostenX's Reputation Management Engine alongside the AI Marketing Automation module:
- **Fake Review Detection AI** scanned all existing reviews and identified 184 reviews (92%) as fraudulent based on pattern analysis, posting behavior, and content fingerprinting
- **Automated Flagging Workflows** submitted removal requests to platforms with AI-generated evidence packages
- **Real-Time Monitoring** established continuous scanning across 50+ sources to catch new fake reviews within hours
- **AI Marketing Campaigns** were launched simultaneously to drive genuine client testimonials and positive organic reviews

### Results
| Metric | Before BoostenX | After 90 Days |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|
| Fake reviews identified | Unknown | 184 flagged (92% of negatives) |
| Reviews removed | 0 | 156 successfully removed |
| Trust score | 2.1/5 | 4.2/5 |
| New client inquiries | ~40/month | ~95/month |
| Manual monitoring hours | 15 hrs/week | 2 hrs/week |

> *"BoostenX transformed our online reputation in under 90 days. The AI detection accuracy was remarkable — it caught patterns our team never would have identified manually."* — Head of Digital, Client Company
