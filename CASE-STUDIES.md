## Case Study 1: 92% Fake Review Removal for Regulated Forex Broker

**Client:** Regulated forex brokerage, operating under Tier-1 jurisdiction license
**Region:** UAE / International
**Engagement:** Online Reputation Management
**Duration:** 30 days
**Year:** 2025

### The Problem

The client, a regulated forex broker with over 50,000 active traders, experienced a coordinated fake review campaign that reduced their Trustpilot score from 4.4 to 2.1 stars within three weeks. Analysis identified hundreds of inauthentic reviews posted in rapid succession from accounts with minimal history — a clear indicator of coordinated attack.

The impact was immediate and measurable: conversion rates from the broker's main landing pages dropped 34% as new prospects encountered the degraded review score.

### BoostenX's Approach

BoostenX's ORM team deployed the platform's NLP classification model against all negative reviews, generating authenticity confidence scores for each. Reviews scoring above the platform's inauthentic threshold were queued for dispute.

Documentation packages were assembled for each inauthentic review, including:
- Account creation timeline analysis
- Review pattern correlation with other coordinated attacks
- IP and device fingerprint patterns (where available)
- Platform policy violation classifications

Disputes were submitted systematically across Trustpilot, Google My Business, and App Store platforms.

Simultaneously, BoostenX launched a verified review generation campaign targeting the broker's existing satisfied client base.

### Results

| Metric | Before | After (30 Days) |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Fake reviews removed | — | 92% of identified inauthentic reviews |
| Trustpilot score | 2.1 stars | 4.1 stars |
| New landing page conversion | -34% (impact period) | Restored to baseline |
| Verified review count | 142 | 289 |

The 92% removal rate was independently confirmed by the client's compliance team, who reviewed the dispute documentation.

## Case Study 3: First-Page SERP Recovery for Fintech Brand

**Client:** B2B fintech company, Series B stage
**Region:** Singapore / APAC
**Engagement:** Search Results Recovery
**Duration:** 90 days
**Year:** 2025

### The Problem

A competitor had commissioned a coordinated negative content campaign targeting the client, resulting in two first-page Google results for the brand's primary keyword being negative forum threads and an uncorrected negative review article. The client's own website ranked third, behind the negative properties.

### BoostenX's Approach

BoostenX conducted a full SERP audit for 12 target keywords and deployed a multi-channel recovery strategy:

**Technical:** Structured data markup on all brand-owned properties to improve eligibility for rich results and knowledge panels.

**Content Syndication:** Publication of in-depth, factual content across DA 70+ platforms including industry publications, professional networks, and verified review platforms.

**Digital PR:** Three earned media placements in regional fintech publications covering the client's funding round and product launch.

**Directory & Platform Optimization:** Crunchbase, LinkedIn company page, Bloomberg profile, and industry directories fully optimized with consistent, accurate information.

### Results

| Keyword | Position Before | Position After (90 Days) |
|---------|-----------------|--------------------------|
| [Brand name] | 3 (own site) | **1 (own site)** |
| [Brand name] review | 1 (negative) | 1 (positive) |
| [Brand name] company | 2 (negative forum) | 1 (own site) |
| [Brand name] legit | 1 (negative) | 1 (positive) |

All four target keywords achieved first-page positive positioning. The negative forum threads dropped to page 2 and beyond.
